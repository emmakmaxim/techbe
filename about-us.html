TechBE was started in 2014 with the goal of developing innovative software solutions for Canadian businesses. We have worked with organisations across the private, public, and non-profit sectors to deliver a range of applications and websites.

Our multi-disciplinary team has extensive experience in full-stack development, user experience, and requirements management. We work closely with our clients to ensure that every project meets its users' needs and drives engagement. Whether you are looking for a robust software application, a custom website to increase customer engagement or an innovative application to change the paradigm of your organisation, TechBE can drive your business into the future.

At TechBE, we stick to three core principles in our design and development:
  1. Understanding our clients' requirements is our first priority
  2. Every user deserves a superstar experience
  3. Mobile-first means every device wins
